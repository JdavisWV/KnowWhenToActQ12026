Segment 3 · What Good Looks Like

Strong vs. Weak Execution

These aren't personality traits. They're behaviors. Behaviors can be changed with a standard.

Weak Escalation
✗Escalating without attempting anything first — dropping it up
✗No context — just forwarding the problem with no framing
✗CCing leadership on every thread to avoid accountability
✗Holding too long and letting a problem become a crisis
✗Escalating to avoid a hard conversation with a peer
Strong Escalation
✓Showing what you tried before asking for help
✓Framing the risk clearly: "If this isn't resolved by X, here's the impact"
✓Going to the right person directly, not broadcasting
✓Naming the risk: "If we don't act today, here's what the client experiences"
✓Following up in writing after a verbal escalation so there's a record
